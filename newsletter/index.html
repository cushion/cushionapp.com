My name is Jonnie and I’m building an app for freelancers. I’m a freelance designer/dev myself and have always had trouble planning and managing the year. I constantly take on too much work and never think to take a break. It’s unhealthy.

I don’t need a complex accounting app or another time-tracker—just a tool to provide better insight. It should warn me when I’m overbooking, so deadlines don’t align to form the perfect storm. It should give me a good idea of how long I can coast based on my monthly expenses. It should bring a calm to the everyday rollercoaster ride that is freelancing.
